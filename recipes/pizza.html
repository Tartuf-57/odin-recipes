Neapolitan Pizza


Description:

Neapolitan pizza originated in Naples, Italy, hundreds of years ago. There is even an Italian non-profit organization—the True Neapolitan Pizza Association (Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana), or AVPN—which is dedicated entirely to protecting the integrity and tradition that go into an authentic Neapolitan pizza.


Ingridients:

  • All purpose flour
  • Dry Yeast
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh Basil
  • Fresh Mozerella
  • Canned Tomatoes

How To Make Neapolitan Pizza

  1. Make the dough: Mix flour, water, salt, and yeast together until the desired consistency is achieved. Then roll out into a disk.
  2. Make the sauce: or slice your tomatoes, simmer with olive oil, and season appropriately while remembering that the beauty of this dish is simplicity.
  3. Assemble the pizza: Spread your sauce and space mozzarella slices evenly around the surface area.
  4. Bake the pizza: dictates that the cooking should be done in a high-heat oven for a very short period of time. It should also be cooked exclusively in a wood-fired oven.
  5. Enjoy: Garnish with fresh basil.
